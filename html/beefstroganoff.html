Beef Stroganoff

Creamy and Delcious Beef Stroganoff

Originally created in Russia, beef stroganoff is made with saut√©ed pieces of beef served in a rich, creamy sauce and served over noodles. Creamy gravy, pefectly cooked steak and sauteed mushrooms to die for!

Ingredients

  • 1 bag egg noodles
  • 1 pound steak
  • 1 condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 tbls garlic powder
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Cook Steak to Perfection
  2. Slice steak up into thin slices and set aside
  3. Meanwhile boil your egg noodles until your liking
  4. Mix soup, garlic, and sour cream in sauce pan until simmer
  5. add steak and cooked noodles
  6. remove from heat and serve
